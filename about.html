About me

My journey began as a Product Manager, where I honed my skills in understanding market needs, defining product strategies, and leading teams to deliver successful products. Over time, I delved deeper into UX/UI design, recognizing the crucial role it plays in creating intuitive and engaging digital experiences.

This combined knowledge in product management and UX/UI design uniquely positions me as a versatile professional. As a Product Owner, I bring a strategic mindset to the table, ensuring that every decision aligns with business goals and user needs. Simultaneously, my UX/UI expertise allows me to translate those strategies into visually appealing and user-friendly designs.

By seamlessly integrating product management principles with user-centric design practices, I strive to create products that not only meet user expectations but also drive business growth.
